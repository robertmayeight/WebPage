The most important things to take away from this module are:

  1. Voltage drop occurs across any component in a circuit.
    2.  
  2. An open switch will drop maximum voltage, because it's resistance is infinite.
    3.  
  3. Components that are not carrying current, are seen as an electrical short to the meter.
    4.  
  4. Meter lead potential, is determined by the first component encountered, that either has current flow, or is the voltage source.
    5.  
  5. A component that doesn't have current flowing through it, does not affect circuit resistance and does not produce a voltage drop.
